Mole A To Mole C Ratio

In the reaction below, how many moles of C can be produced from 3.28 moles
3A + 3B  ⇒  3C + D



We already have our balanced equation, so let's find our answer by doing a mole to mole ratio

3.28 moles A
(3 moles C) / (3 moles A)
= 3.28 moles C



3.28 moles of A will produce 3.28 moles of C
