Finding Volume Of 90.5 moles Ideal Gas

If 90.5 moles of an ideal gas is at 1.77 atm at 34.80 °C,
what is the volume of the gas?



Applying the Ideal Gas Law, we first want to check that all units match R 0.082057 L atm mol-1 K-1

We will have to convert our temperature value
34.80 °C + 273.15 = 307.95 K

Now, we're ready for pV = nRT

(1.77 atm)(x) = (90.5 moles)
(0.082057 L atm) /         mol-1 K-1
(307.95 K)
