Calculating Density Of 2.37 L Gas

The mass of 2.37 L of a gas is 8.91 g. What is the density of the gas?

A. 21.1 g/L
B. 3.76 g/L
C. 6.54 g/L
D. 0.266 g/L
E. None of these



Density (d) =
Mass (g) / Volume (L)


d =
8.91 g / 2.37 L


d = 3.7594
g / L


considering sig figs
d = 3.76
g / L


Option B is the right answer to this question
