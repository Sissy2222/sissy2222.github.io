Converting 4.96 Grams B2O3 To Moles

Find the number of moles in 4.96 g of B2O3?



First we want to find the molecular weight of diborontrioxide

Boron = 2(10.81 g/mole) = 21.62 g/mole
Oxide = 3(16.00 g/mole) = 48.00 g/mole /                                            69.62 g/mole



and now, we convert

4.96 g B2O3
(1 mole B2O3) / (69.62 g B2O3)
= 0.0712 moles B2O3



The number of B2O3 moles is 0.0712
