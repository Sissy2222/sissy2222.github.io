Converting 2.5 Gallons H2O To Grams

What is the mass in grams of 2.50 gal of water?



First, let's list all necessary conversion factors

1 gal = 3.78541 L
1000 mL = 1 L
1 mL = 1 g


and now we convert

2.5 gal H2O
(3.78541 L H2O) / (1 gal H2O)
(1000 mL H2O) / (1 L H2O)
(1 g H2O) / (1 mL H2O)
= 9 463.53 g H2O


The mass of 2.50 gallons of water is 9.46 x 103 g
