4.59 moles O2 In Grams

What is the mass, in grams, of 4.59 moles of oxygen gas?



To answer this question, we'll convert moles oxygen to grams oxygen using molecular weight.
The key is to recognize that we're dealing with O2, not an oxide (just O)

(4.59 moles O2) / 1
 
(32.00 g O2) / (1 mole O2)
  = 146.88 g O2
