4.2 x 1024 Atoms Sulfur To Grams

How many grams are 4.2 x 1024 atoms of sulfur?



To answer this question,
we want to use Avogadro's number to convert atoms to moles,
then we'll use molecular weight to convert moles to grams


4.2 x 1024 atoms S
(1 mole S) / (6.022 x 1023 atoms S)
(32.06 g S) / (1 mole S)
= 223.59 g S



4.2 x 1024 atoms of sulfur = 2.2 x 102 g S
