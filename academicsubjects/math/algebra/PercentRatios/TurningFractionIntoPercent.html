2/5 in Percent

What is 2/5 in percentage form?



First, we want to turn the fraction into a decimal
there are two ways of doing this

either treating the fraction as division,
since this is what a fraction represents
2 / 5
= 2 : 5 = 0.4

Percentage

or manipulating the fraction to have a
denominator consisting of multiples of 10

2 / 5
=
4 / 10
= 0.4

To get rid of a percentage sign, we divide by 100,
therefore,
to add the %, we multiply by 100

0.4(100) = 40%
