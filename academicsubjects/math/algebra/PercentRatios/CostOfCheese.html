Cost Of 100 g Cheese

A 450-g package of cheese costs $8.73.
What is the cost of cheese per 100 g?



The best way to solve this is by setting up a ratio

450 g = $8.73 therefore, 100 g = ?

450 g / 100 g
=
$8.73 / $x


I like to have my variable (x) on the left side in the numerator,
so, let's flip this

$x / 100 g
=
$8.73 / 450 g


and now, we solve for x

x =
($8.73)(100 g) / 450 g


x = $1.94

100 g of this cheese will cost $1.94
