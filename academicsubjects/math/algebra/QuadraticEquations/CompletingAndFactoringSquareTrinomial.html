Complete 9x2 + ___x + 144 & Factor

What number has to fill in the blank to make this a perfect square trinomial
9x2 + ___x + 144
Write this square trinomial in factored form



to find the middle term, we want to use the generic quadratic equation

a2 + 2ab + b2

9x2 + ____ + 144

to find the value of a and b, let's take the square root of each

√ 9x2 = 3x

√ 144 = 12


2ab = 2(3x)(12) = 72x

The middle term is 72 x


to factor this trinomial, we want to recognize that each coefficient is divisible by 9
so let's factor out that 9

9x2 + 72x + 144

9(x2 + 8x + 16)

looks like we have a perfect square
what can be multiplied to get 16 and ± to get 8
the answer is, of course, 4

9(x2 + 4x + 4x + 16)


factored form = 9(x + 4)2
