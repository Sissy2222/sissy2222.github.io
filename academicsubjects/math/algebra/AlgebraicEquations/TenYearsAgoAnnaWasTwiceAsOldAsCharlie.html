Ten Years Ago Anna Was Twice As Old As Charlie

Ten years ago, Anna was twice as old as Charlie
3 years ago, 4 times Megans age was 3 times Charlies age today
In 15 years, Anna is 8 years older than Megan
How old are Anna, Megan & Charlie?



Let's take this step by step

Ten years ago, Anna was twice as old as Charlie

Ten years ago
A - 10 = C - 10
Anna was twice Charlies age, so we multiply Charlies age by 2
A - 10 = 2(C - 10)


3 years ago, 4 times Megans age was 3 times Charlies age today

4 times Megans age was 3 times Charlies age
4M = 3C
and that was Megans age 3 years ago
4M - 3 = 3C


in 15 years, Anna is 8 years older than Megan
A + 15 = M + 15 + 8


This leaves us with three equations

A - 10 = 2(C - 10)
4M - 3 = 3C
A + 15 = M + 15 + 8

Rearranging these, we'll get

A - 2C = - 10
4M - 3C = 3
A - M = 8



Now, we treat these as a system of equations
