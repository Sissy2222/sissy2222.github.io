Diaphragm

Meaning


dia = across
phram = fence

Function


contraction = inhalation
relaxing = exhalation

Origin


lower six ribs,
lower spine and
xyphoid process (sternum)

Insertion


central tendon

spaceholder

_________________________

External oblique

Meaning


ex = out of
external = on outside
oblique = slanted,
sidelong

Function


rotate trunk, supports
abdominal wall,
compresses the
abdominal cavity,
abducts and rotates
trunk

Origin


lower eight ribs

Insertion


iliac crest, rectus
sheath, pubic
crest & ligament


_________________________

Iliocostalis cervicis

Meaning


ilium = groin of flank
costa = rib
cervix = neck

Function


extends, hyper-extends
and flexes cervical vertebrae

Origin


ribs 3 - 6

Insertion


vertebrae C4 - C6

_________________________

Iliocostalis thoracis

Meaning


ilium = groin of flank
costa = rib
thorax = chest, chest plate
digitorum = digitus =
pointing thing
longus = long


Function


extends & flexes spine

Origin


iliocostalis lumborum
and ribs 7 - 12

Insertion


ribs 1 - 6 & C7

_________________________

Internal oblique

Meaning


internal = on inside
oblique = slanted, sidelong

Function


abducts and rotates
trunk, supports abdominal
wall, helps forced
respiration & raising
intra-abdominal pressure

Origin


lumbar fascia, groin
ligaments and iliac crest

Insertion


pubic crest, pectineal
line, rectus sheath and
bottom edge of the rib cage

_________________________

Latissimus dorsi

Meaning


latissimus = broadest
dorsi = back

Function


extents, adducts &
rotates arm

Origin


lumbar fascia, iliac
crest, spinous
processes T7 - T12,
last four ribs
and lesser scapula angle

Insertion


bicipital groove of humerus

_________________________

Pectoralis major

Meaning


pectus = breast
major = great in size or importance

Function


flexes, adducts
and rotates arm

Origin


inner clavicle, outer
sternum, first six
ribs and white
fibrous tissue of
external oblique

Insertion


bicipital humeral
groove and deltoid
tuberosity

_________________________

Pectoralis minor

Meaning


pectus = breeast
minor = lesser


Function


elevates 3rd - 5th rib (inhalation)
and protracts (extends) scapula

Origin


3rd, 4th & 5th rib

Insertion


coracoid process (scapula)

_________________________

Quadratus lumborum

Meaning


quadrus = square
lumbus = loin

Function


flexes hip, and
adducts & rotates thigh

Origin


iliac crest, iliolumbar
ligament and apex of L1-4

Insertion


lower border of 12thrib

_________________________

Rectus abdominis

Meaning


rectus = straight
abdom = lower venter, belly


Function


flexes spinal column and
increase intra-abdominal pressure

Origin


pubic symphysis

Insertion


ribs 5 - 7 and
xiphoid process (sternum)

_________________________

Rhomboid major

Meaning


rhomboid = rhombus
major = great in size or importance

Function


retracts & rotate
scapula

Origin


supraspinous
ligament and
spinous T2 - T5
processes

Insertion


lower border of
scapula and base
of scapular spine

_________________________

Rhomboid minor

Meaning


rhomboid = rhombus
minor = lesser

Function


retracts & rotate
scapula

Origin


nuchea and spines
of C7 & T1

Insertion


border of scapula

_________________________

Serratus anterior

Meaning


serratus = saw shaped
anterior = front, fore

Function


rotates & protracts
scapula

Origin


external oblique, upper
eight ribs and midclavicular
line membrane

Insertion


inner border of scapula

_________________________

Transversus abdominis


 >

   Meaning


transversus = crosswise
abdom = lower venter, belly

Function


supports abdominal
wall and increases
the intraabdominal
pressure

Origin


lumbar fascia, iliac
crest and inguinal
ligament

Insertion


fibrous tissue of
rectus sheath,
pubic crest and
pectineal line

_________________________

Trapezius

Meaning


trapezoid shaped

Function


rotates, elevates
and retracts
shoulder blades,
flexes neck

Origin


nuchal line, back of
neck (nuchae), spinous
processes and T12 ligament

Insertion


clavicle, scapula & acromion

_________________________

Psoas major

Meaning


psoas = muscle of the loin
major = great in size or importance

Function


flexes & rotates hip

Origin


L1-5, L1-5 & T12-L4

Insertion


lower trochanter of femur

_________________________

Iliacus

Meaning


ilium = flank or groin

Function


flexes and externally
rotates the femur,
hip flexor

Origin


iliac fossa

Insertion


lower trochanter of femur

_________________________

Gluteus maximus

Meaning


gloutos = the rump
maximus = great
outermost muscle
of the buttocks


Function


extends & rotates hip

Origin


coccyx (tailbone),
sacrum and ilium

Insertion


gluteal tuberosity
(rounded prominence
on bone) of
femur and iliotibial
tract

_________________________

Gluteus medius

Meaning


gloutos = the rump
medius = middle

Function


abducts hip joint

Origin


between back and
middle gluteal
lines of ilieum

Insertion


trocanter of femur
