Iron To Sodium

How many moles of sodium metal is obtained when 36.0 moles of iron metal completely reacts with sodium bromide?

Fe + 3 NaBr  ⇒  FeBr3 + 3 Na



We already have our balanced equation, so let's do a mole to mole ratio to get our answer

36.0moles Fe
(3 moles Na) /(1 mole Fe)
= 108 moles Na



36.0 moles iron will produce 108 mole of sodium
