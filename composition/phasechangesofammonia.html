Phase Changes Of Ammonia

Would ammonia be a solid, liquid or gas at

a) - 55 °C
b) - 17 °C
c) - 113 °C



Let's start by taking a close look at ammonias boiling- and melting points


boiling point is the temperature
at which the compound changes
from liquid to gas

boiling point of NH3 = - 33.34 °C

 phase change of ammonia

melting point is the temperature
at which the compound changes
from solid to liquid

melting point of NH3 = - 77.73 °C





Answers

at - 55 °C NH3 is a liquid

at - 17 °C NH3 is a gas

at - 113 °C NH3 is a solid
