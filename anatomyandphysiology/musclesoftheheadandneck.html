Anterior scalenes

Meaning


antero = in front
scalene = uneven, unequal
anterior scalene bends neck

Function


flexes & bends neck
elevates 1st rib

Origin


transverse processes of C3 - 6

Insertion


proturbence of 1st rib

spaceholder

_________________________

Levator scapulae

Meaning


levator = lift
scapula = shoulder,
shoulder blade

Function


elevates (and rotates)
scapula

Origin


transverse processes
of C1 - 4

Insertion


middle of upper scapula border
femoral condyle


_________________________

Platysma

Meaning


platysma = flat plate


Function


depresses mandible and
lower face (mouth - frowning)

Origin


lower neck and
upper chest

Insertion


lower jaw,
skin of lower face and
angles of mouth

_________________________

Sternohyoid

Meaning


sternon = flat surface
(breast, chest, breastbone)
hyoid = shaped like a 'U'

Function


depressess hyoid bone

Origin


right & left sternohoid
attach the hyoid bone to sternum

Insertion


small mandible border
and lower face
(corners of mouth)

_________________________

Sternocleidomasto

Meaning


sternon = flat surface (breast, chest, breastbone)
cleido = clavicle
mastos = breast shaped

Function


flexes & rotates
cervical spine,
helps elongate (stretch) neck

Origin


manubrium and middle
third of clavicle

Insertion


mastoid process and
nuchal (nape of neck) line

_________________________

Buccinator

Meaning


bucca = cheek

Function


helps with chewing and
closing mouth,
tenses cheeks (whistling)

Origin


jawbone, sphenoid (front of skull)

Insertion


angle of mouth extending
through orbicularis oris

_________________________

Depressor anguli oris

Meaning


de = from or away
press = squeeze (down)
anguli = angle, corner, apex
oris = mouth, opening


Function


depresses mouth
pulls corners back &
down (frown)

Origin


mandible

Insertion


angle of mouth

_________________________

Depressor labii inferioris

Meaning


de = from or away
press = squeeze (down)
labii = lip
inferior = lower

Function


depresses lower kip

Origin


mandible

Insertion


skin of lower lip

_________________________

Frontalis

Meaning


front = fore


Function


wrinkles forehead
(raises eyebrows)

Origin


mastoid process,
upper facial muscles

Insertion


fibrous tissue of forehead

_________________________

Levator labii superioris

Meaning


levator = lift
labii = lip
superior = above

Function


elevates upper lip

Origin


lower eye socket

Insertion


upper lip

_________________________

Masseter

Meaning


masseter = the chewer

Function


elevates mandible
(pulls lower jaw upwards)

Origin


maxilla
(upper jaw)

Insertion


lower jaw extensions

_________________________

Mentalis

Meaning


mentum = chin

Function


elevates chin,
protrudes lower lip

Origin


jaw fossa

Insertion


skin of chin

_________________________

Nasalis


 >

   Meaning


nasalis = nose

Function


opens & closes nostrils

Origin


upper jaw

Insertion


fibrous tissue of nose

_________________________

Occipitalis

Meaning


occipitus = back of skull

Function


'pulls scalp back'
helps wrinkle forehead

Origin


protrudence of occipital
and temporal bone

Insertion


fibrous tissue of forehead

_________________________

Orbicularis oculi

Meaning


orbis = circle or disk
ulus (ularis) = little
oculus = eye
little circle of the eye

Function


closes eyelids, helps occular drainage

Origin


eye socket and
lacrimal sac

Insertion


frontal ridge of eyelid

_________________________

Orbicularis oris

Meaning


orbis = circle or disk
ulus (ularis) = little
oris = mouth, opening
little circle of the mouth

Function


closes mouth
purses & puckers lips

Origin


upper & lower jaw

Insertion


lips

_________________________

Temporalis

Meaning


tempus = time
covers 'time bone'


Function


moves lower jaw up & down
and side-to-side

Origin


temporal fossa

Insertion


coronoid process of lower jaw

_________________________

Zygomaticus major

Meaning


zygon = yoke
major = greater

Function


draws mouth angles up and out

Origin


zygomatic bone

Insertion


angles of mouth

_________________________

Zygomaticus minor

   Meaning


zygon = yoke
minor = lesser


Function


draws upper lip back, up and out

Origin


lower eye socket

Insertion


upper lip

_________________________

Inferior oblique

Meaning


inferior = lower, farther down
oblique = slanting


Function


elevates eye
moving it up and away
from nose

Origin


upper jaw

Insertion


lower quarter in back
of outer layer of eyeball

_________________________

Inferior rectus

Meaning





inferior = lower, farther down
rectus = straight

Function


depresses eye
moving it up, down and
in towards nose

Origin





lower ring of eyeball

Insertion


lower part in back
of outer layer of eyeball

_________________________

Lateral rectus

Meaning


latteral = belonging to the side
rectus = straight


Function


abducts eye

Origin


lower ring of eyeball

Insertion


lower part in back
of outer layer of eyeball

_________________________

Medial rectus

Meaning


medial = middle, between
rectus = straight


Function


adducts eye

Origin


lower ring of eyeball

Insertion


lower part in back
of outer layer of eyeball

_________________________

Superior oblique

Meaning


superior = higher, upper
oblique = slanting


Function


internal rotation
depresses eye adduction
rotates in abduction

Origin


sphenoid

Insertion


upper quarter in back
of outer layer of eyeball

_________________________

Superior rectus

Meaning


superior = higher, upper
rectus = straight


Function


elevates eye
rotates in adduction

Origin


upper ring of eyeball

Insertion


upper part in back
of outer layer of eyeball
