Reasons why students may fall behind: - Perhaps, the material just did not make sense, because it wasn't explained well.
- Personality conflict between the student and the teacher
- Preoccupied, lacking confidence and are simply overwhelmed
by 'lots and lots of puberty'.
- Indulged in prograstination and did not get back in the saddle on time
- Student only did 'okay' in a previous class onto which
the current subject builds

It is best to nip any rough spots in the bud as soon as they occur. Smart people will work on figuring things out themselves,
but smart people also know, that once you hit the proverbial brickwall, it's time to consult a subject specific expert.
In this case, a professional tutor.

Meeting Places


In person meetings occur at a local library or coffee shop. It is best not to meet at a students home,
because there are too many distractions and the balance between student and tutor is out of sync.

Online meetings occur via google meet (audio & video) and jamboard (online whiteboard).
It is not necessary to have a google workplace account. Once a date/time is set, I will send
an address via email that will allow the student to join the meeting. In case of technical
difficulties with camera and/or microphone, we can use the chatbox in jamboard or simply talk
on the phone will working on the board (this ensures a more fluid engagement).
