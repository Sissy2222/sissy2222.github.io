Additional Info
       
It is best to nip any rough spots in the bud as soon as they occur. This will prevent the vicious cycle of stress, prograstination
and avoidance. Real tutoring is a bit different from learning in a classroom. Here, the objectives are deeper understanding,
assimilation & learning how to become a problem solver. These are key to doing well in any subject.

       
New information is fun, but when the concepts are not explained well, it will lead to non-comprehension

= confusion & stress
  
Often, students are provided with formulas and directions on how to apply these. This can be rewarding, until the parameters change

= apprehension & hesitation
  
Actually understanding the underlying dynamics, the 'Why" it is so and the 'How' it came to be so, changes everything

= zeal, growth & freedom

Meeting Places


In person meetings occur at a local library or coffee shop. It is best not to meet at a students home,
because there are too many distractions and the balance between student and tutor is out of sync.

Online meetings occur via google meet (audio & video) and jamboard (online whiteboard).
It is not necessary to have a google workplace account. Once a date/time is set, I will send
an address via email that will allow the student to join the meeting. In case of technical
difficulties with camera and/or microphone, we can use the chatbox in jamboard or simply talk
on the phone will working on the board (this ensures a more fluid engagement).
