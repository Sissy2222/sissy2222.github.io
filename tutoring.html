Adductor longus

Meaning


adduct = draw toward
longus = long

Function


adducts & flexes
the thigh at the hip

Origin


upper pubis, below
the pubic tubercle

Insertion


in the linear third
of the linea aspera
of the femur

spaceholder

Adductor magnus

Meaning


adduct = to draw toward
magnus = great

Function


adducts hip towards
bodies midline

Origin


outer surface of lower
pubic ramus & ischial ramus

Insertion


at the adductor tubercle
on the medial
femoral condyle


Biceps femoris

Meaning


bicept = double-headed
or two peaks
femoris = femur =
long bone of upper leg

Function


one of three hamstring muscles
extends thigh and
bends & rotates the knee

Origin


back of upper leg (hamstring)
facet of the ischial tuberosity

Insertion


head of the fibula
