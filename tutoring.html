KaffeeKlatsch & Learn

This is the place and time to meet like minded people, put your language skills to good use and pick up new words, phrases & terms.
Learning a language in class/from a book is one thing, carrying a conversation is another story altogether.
A particular sentence may make perfect sense in one language, but can be completely nonsensical in another.
Keep in mind, a countrys' language reflects its culture, its peoples way of thinking and their understanding of the world around them.

As of right now, there are two weekly groups, each meeting either at a local coffeeshop or library.
There will always be a conversation topic, either preset or per request.
