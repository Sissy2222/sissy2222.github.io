management, I finally found my calling... never having been interested in science, I nevertheless (for some

about Jenner, Lister and Pasteur (this was about half way through the book, which I never finished), I
was hooked... The following semester, I (at that time 28 year old single mother) enrolled
in a university and never looked back.
